Group Project:

The objective of the group project is to be able to experience the workflow of working on a codebase in a small team. You should make a brief plan of what you want to build, ideally write down some ideas for layout on the whiteboard or paper to formulate a plan to break up who works on what page and who will commit the beginning of the project.

A few ideas I suggest you choose from are the following. If you would like to pick another idea for the group project run it by me and I will give ita go ahead or not depending on complexity.


Team Group Introduction:

Home page with a sub pages that allows for a description of each member of the team.


Intro to DTech:

This is meant to be a site that described the school, the culture, and the surrounding areas for DTech High School.


Entertainment and Social Media:

A site that describes the platforms of entertainment you use and what to do on them. This can be anything from TV Shows, Netflix, Messenger Platforms, and Video Games.


Life in the Bay Area:

Talk about various aspects of what it feels like living in the Bay Area in this day and age. You can talk about the cities you live in, transportation, communication, and school.
