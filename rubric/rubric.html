Winter Project: 20 Points

For full credit on this assignment you should have at least a single page with CSS on all of your elements. If you have multiple pages you must be able to navigate between each page.

- Relevant Content: 10 Points

- Page Uploaded and Online: 5 Points

- CSS on all Elements: 5 Points

Pull Request: 10 Points

For this assignment you need to create code in a branch, create a pull request on github.com, and merge you code into master.

Team Project: 25 Points

To receive full credit for this assignment you must add a single page to your group website that fits the style of your homepage. The stylesheets of the project should be shared across all pages. As this is a group project it will be expected for group memebers to communicate respectfully to eachother.

- Contributed to Project: 10 Points

- Used Shared Stylesheets: 5 Points

- Submitted work to master via Pull Request: 5 Points

- Professionalism: 5 Points

Post Mortem: 5 Points

Each team will compile a brief Post Mortem on their group project. To recieve credit each member must contribute their feedback to the team.

Cover Letter: 10 Points

For this assigment you must follow the format for what is expected in a cover letter to send to a company of your choice. When it is ready send me a shared link to the doc with commenting privileges for feedback. The Cover Letter must be free of spelling or grammatical errors to receive full credit.

Personal Tasks: 30 Points

In week two I will have a list of projects for you to choose from that vary in point values. You can choose any number of projects complete and turn them in when ready. The list of projects will be posted in Week 2 along with their point values.
