Status Update: 15 Points

For full credit on this assignment you should have at least a single page with CSS on all of your elements. If you have multiple pages you must be able to navigate between each page.

- Relevant Content: 4 Points

- Page Uploaded and Online: 3 Points

- CSS on all Elements: 3 Points

- Daily Commitment: 5 Points

Team Planning: 20 Points

For this assignment each team will make a plan of what your group website will be and who will be responsible for what portions. At the end of the day you should have a breakdown of every page you will need, and who will be responsible for each section. You should plan for 2.5 days worth of work, with 3 hours considered a day.

- Contributed to Planning: 5 Points

- Signed up for Tasks: 5 Points

- Paper/Digital Layout of Required Pages/CSS/Javascript: 5 Points

- Professionalism: 5 Points

Photoshop: 10 Points

Worth a total of 5 points per day, each student should be actively trying to build using the Adobe Creative Suite. You should use this time to become accustomed to using the toolset and perhaps build some of the art work for your group project or personal projects.

Group Project: 40 Points

This will be the actual work done to build your project. Each team is expeccted to perform a daily standup and select which task you will do. These tasks should be the ones you generated during your planning phase the previous week. Each team will send daily updates to their commitments as well as an end of day status update with what each individual accomplished. The final product should have all pages working and accessable. No broken CSS or 404s, and hosted online.

- Daily Checkins: 15 Points

- Professionalism: 15 Points

- Completed Product: 5 Points

- Presentation: 5 Points

Job & Salary Hunt: 15 Points

I will pass out a document where each student is meant to go over looking at jobs and job posts and the salary associated with them.

- Job Postings: 10 Points

- Salary Ranges: 5 Points
